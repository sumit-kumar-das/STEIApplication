Systems Technician (ST) and Electrical Installer (EI) Employee Planner

Welcome to the Johnson Controls System Technician and Electrical Installer Onboarding program.



This is a 4 week program which will teach you about the company, your role and related technical trainings.

The tasks are divided into modules and assignments. You will be able to view and track your progress on completion of these tasks on a daily and weekly basis.
